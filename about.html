About Manpasand Chicken

Fresh • Hygienic • Trusted Local Shop

Who We Are

Manpasand Chicken is a trusted local shop serving fresh poultry chicken, fish, and eggs to nearby families. We believe in quality, hygiene, and freshness — delivered with honesty.

Our Story

Started as a small neighborhood shop, Manpasand Chicken was built on one simple mission — to provide clean, fresh, and fairly priced food products. Over time, we earned the trust of our local customers through consistency and care.

Why Choose Us

  • Hygienic handling & clean workspace
  • Trusted by local customers
  • Fair pricing with no quality compromise
  • Quick pickup & home delivery

Our Products

Our Promise

We follow proper cleaning, storage, and cutting practices. Your health, satisfaction, and trust are always our top priority.
